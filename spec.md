# Content Factory Spec

## Product Type
ebook

## Topic & Angle
A practical playbook for solo founders to launch their first AI-assisted digital product in 14 days using a repeatable workflow (idea validation, offer design, content production, and launch execution).

## Target Audience
- Who: Solo founders and indie makers (0-3 years in business) selling online education or digital downloads
- Already knows: Basic audience research, simple landing page setup, and how to use ChatGPT/Claude at a beginner level
- Needs: A step-by-step system that turns a rough idea into a market-ready knowledge product quickly without hiring a team
- Willing to pay: $29-$99 for a tactical guide with templates and checklists

## Tone & Voice
- Style: Friendly-expert and tactical
- Person: Second person
- Reading level: grade 8

## Competitive Landscape
- Existing products: Creator MBA launch guides, Gumroad creator playbooks, generic "AI content" ebooks
- Differentiation: More operational and time-boxed; includes explicit day-by-day execution steps, decision gates, and lightweight QA checks for shipping

## Hard Constraints
- Length: 9,000-12,000 words
- Sections: 8-10
- Formats: markdown, pdf
- Brand: Clean, high-contrast formatting with simple callout boxes and checklist styling

## Deliverables
1. Main deliverable: 30-40 page ebook in Markdown and PDF
2. Bonus deliverable: 14-day launch checklist (print-friendly)
3. Supporting asset: Offer and messaging worksheet (fillable Markdown template)
4. Supporting asset: Curated references/resources appendix

## Quality Thresholds
- Minimum gate confidence: 0.85
- Readability target: Flesch-Kincaid grade 8-10
- Max retry cycles per gate: 3

## Done When
- [ ] All deliverables exist as final files
- [ ] No placeholder text anywhere
- [ ] Table of contents matches actual content
- [ ] All internal cross-references resolve
- [ ] Formatting is consistent throughout
- [ ] Spell check and grammar check pass
- [ ] All automated QA gates passed at or above confidence threshold
- [ ] Export renders correctly in target format(s)
