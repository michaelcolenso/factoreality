# Launch Blueprint: Medical Bill Negotiation & Dispute Kit

## 1) Positioning Statement
For U.S. patients overwhelmed by medical bills, this product is a practical self-advocacy kit that shows exactly what to say, write, and do to reduce or dispute costs, access aid, and protect credit. Unlike fragmented blog posts or expensive advocates, it provides an integrated, script-driven workflow at a consumer price.

## 2) Buyer Transformation Promise (Outcome Ladder)
1. **Stabilize emotions and timeline**: stop panic payments and create a paper trail.
2. **Find leverage**: detect bill errors, coding mismatches, and unsupported charges.
3. **Create negotiation power**: compare billed charges vs reasonable benchmarks and financial hardship status.
4. **Reduce liability**: secure corrections, discounts, charity care, settlements, and fair payment plans.
5. **Prevent downstream damage**: protect credit and properly respond to collections.

## 3) Offer Stack (Value Anchored)
- **Standard ($39):** Core guide + audit checklist + key templates.
- **Pro Toolkit ($67):** Everything in Standard + full scripts + expanded letters.
- **Complete Defense ($97):** Everything in Pro + advanced collections and appeals playbooks.
- **Order bump / upsells ($29 each):**
  - Insurance Denial Appeal Deep Dive
  - Collection Defense Pack

### Value Messaging by Tier
- **$39**: “Understand your bill and stop overpaying.”
- **$67**: “Get done-for-you language for hard conversations.”
- **$97**: “Operate like a full consumer-defense system.”

## 4) Subagent-Orchestrated Delivery Plan
The launch should be executed as a controlled sprint with clear owners and handoffs.

### Phase A — Product Finalization (Week 1)
- **Research Agent**: validate legal/program references, state/federal caveats, and top objections.
- **Content Agent**: finalize all core modules and examples.
- **Template Agent**: produce all scripts, letters, and checklists in plain-language format.
- **QA Reviewer Agent**: run rubric scoring for completeness, readability, and actionability.

### Phase B — Conversion Infrastructure (Week 2)
- **Offer Agent**: finalize pricing page copy and tier naming.
- **Copy Agent**: sales page sections, FAQ, guarantee language, and CTA hierarchy.
- **Design Agent**: page visual hierarchy, trust badges, comparison grid, and checkout UX.
- **Analytics Agent**: event tracking and dashboard baseline.

### Phase C — Traffic Activation (Weeks 3-4)
- **SEO Agent**: publish 3 pillar posts + 7 cluster posts.
- **Short-Form Agent**: publish 20 clips with hooks, proof, and CTA.
- **Community Agent**: educational posting cadence for Reddit/Facebook.
- **Paid Agent**: launch controlled high-intent search campaigns.

## 5) Funnel Architecture
1. **Top of funnel**: SEO + short-form educational content.
2. **Lead capture**: free checklist + “bill panic triage” cheatsheet.
3. **Nurture**: 7-email authority and implementation sequence.
4. **Sales conversion**: tiered checkout and one-click upsell.
5. **Activation**: onboarding milestones + “first 48 hours” quick-start checklist.
6. **Ascension**: upsells and optional partner referrals (e.g., nonprofit aid links).

## 6) Sales Page Requirements
- Hero: clear promise + audience match
- Problem section: debt stress and system opacity
- Mechanism section: explain the 7-step framework
- What’s included: asset grid with format and use-case labels
- Tier table: $39 vs $67 vs $97 with “best for” guidance
- Credibility: sourced stats, process transparency, and realistic claim framing
- FAQ: legality, timelines, likelihood of success, who it is/isn’t for
- CTA strategy: first CTA above fold + repeated contextual CTAs every major section

## 7) Content Engine (First 30 Days)
- 10 SEO articles targeting high-intent keywords.
- 20 short-form videos (myth busting + “exact phrase to say” clips).
- 8 Reddit/Facebook educational posts with non-spam value-first formatting.
- 7-email nurture sequence (problem agitation -> authority -> proof -> offer).

## 8) Success Metrics (Targets)
- Landing page conversion: 2.5-5%
- Front-end average order value (AOV): $55+
- Upsell take rate: 10-20%
- Refund rate: <5%
- Email opt-in to purchase: 3-8%
- Time-to-first-action after purchase: <24 hours for 60%+ of customers

## 9) Operational Cadence
- **Daily**: monitor traffic, CPA, and checkout conversion.
- **Weekly**: review funnel drop-off and test one offer/copy hypothesis.
- **Bi-weekly**: refresh top-performing scripts and add one new objection-handler template.
- **Monthly**: publish outcome report (anonymized wins + implementation insights).
