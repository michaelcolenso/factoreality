# Launch Blueprint: Medical Bill Negotiation & Dispute Kit

## 1) Positioning Statement
For U.S. patients overwhelmed by medical bills, this product is a practical self-advocacy kit that shows exactly what to say, write, and do to reduce or dispute costs, access aid, and protect credit. Unlike fragmented blog posts or expensive advocates, it provides an integrated, script-driven workflow at a consumer price.

## 2) Core Promise & Proof
- Primary transformation: “From fear and confusion to a documented, strategic action plan that can lower bill burden.”
- Proof mechanisms:
  - Data-led context (error rates, debt prevalence, denial appeal wins)
  - Before/after case-style examples (anonymized)
  - Script snippets and checklist previews to demonstrate concrete utility

## 3) Offer Stack
- **Standard ($39):** Core guide + audit checklist + key templates.
- **Pro Toolkit ($67):** Everything in Standard + full scripts + expanded letters.
- **Complete Defense ($97):** Everything in Pro + advanced collections and appeals playbooks.
- **Order bump / upsells ($29):**
  - Insurance Denial Appeal Deep Dive
  - Collection Defense Pack

## 4) Funnel Architecture
1. **Top of funnel** (awareness): SEO articles and short-form videos.
2. **Mid funnel** (consideration): lead magnet (“5-costly medical bill mistakes”) + email nurture.
3. **Bottom funnel** (conversion): sales page with tier comparison + FAQ + risk-reversal policy.
4. **Post-purchase**: one-click upsell, then onboarding email sequence with implementation milestones.

## 5) Content Engine (First 30 Days)
- 10 SEO articles targeting high-intent keywords.
- 20 short-form videos (myth busting + “exact phrase to say” clips).
- 8 Reddit/Facebook educational posts with non-spam value-first formatting.
- 7-email nurture sequence (problem agitation → authority → proof → offer).

## 6) Sales Page Requirements
- Hero: clear promise + audience match
- Problem section: debt stress and system opacity
- Solution section: 7-step framework visual
- What’s included: assets and templates grid
- Tier table: $39 vs $67 vs $97
- Credibility: data citations + case narratives + transparent disclaimers
- FAQ: legality, timelines, likely outcomes, who it is/isn’t for

## 7) Success Metrics
- Landing page conversion: 2.5-5%
- Front-end average order value (AOV): $55+
- Upsell take rate: 10-20%
- Refund rate: <5%
- Email opt-in to purchase: 3-8%

## 8) Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Finalize core product files and templates
- [ ] Build sales page and checkout tiers
- [ ] Publish first 3 SEO pages and 5 short videos
- [ ] Launch lead magnet + email automation
- [ ] Activate paid search pilot (limited daily budget)
- [ ] Review conversion data weekly and iterate offer messaging
