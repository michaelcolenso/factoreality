# Product Brief Template

Use this when you want the orchestrator to auto-generate `spec.md`.

## Product idea
Describe the product you want to create in 2-4 sentences.

## Audience
Who it is for, what they currently struggle with, and their baseline skill level.

## Outcome
What concrete result the buyer should achieve after using this product.

## Constraints (optional)
Any limits on length, formats, timeline, or voice.

## Deliverables (optional)
List must-have outputs (for example: guide, checklist, worksheet, templates).
